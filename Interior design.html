Certificate courses on Interior Design

Interior design is a process of enhancing comfort living indoor space. Well-designed interior space insures spatial comfort and visual comfort. Any space should be designed for the users’ every single activity. As a designer it’s a great challenge to house every need of the users within the assigned space. Interior designing is a field which demands creativity, passion & hard work. One with excellent design ability, exposure on current materials, ability to work with estimated quote, can excel in interior designing profession.

Career Options and Job Prospects

Interior design field is a multi-faceted field, one has opportunity around. Most of the industry sub-fields in construction is related to architecture interior design. Any construction or part of construction always terminates with services extended by interior designers. Considering the need that is thriving we are adding another wing our esac institute. Interior designers are needed for design and execution of interiors of all spaces like residence, shops, hotels, hospitals, boutiques, etc. Interior designers have job options with architects firms, town planning firms, interior contactor, real estate offices, specific branded stores, modular kitchen, etc. One can work in any of the interior work related firm or can start own office and run his/her own business.

COURSES OFFERED

⦁ Foundation course Interior Design – 3 Semester + 1 month project - Total 10 months
⦁ Advanced Interior Design – 4 Semester + 2 month project - Total 1 year 2 months

Interior Designing Eligibility Criteria

⦁ Min Qualification Required: 10+2 in any discipline for the foundation course.
⦁ Any bachelor degree (preferably any design related Ug) for Advance Interior Design course.
⦁ Min Marks Required: At least 50% in class 12.



Material Library:

Esac has an extensive collection of both interior and exterior materials. Variety of materials on Façade finishes including HPL, ACP, Stone cladding, paints, etc. Materials for different types of flooring, wall paneling, partition walls, false ceiling, etc. are available. Fittings and fixtures of doors, windows, cabinets, drawers, etc. All possible channels and cross sections are also available. The material library also contains fabric samples of curtains, carpets, etc. The material library helps students feel the materials and understand its visual & tactile properties. One can also know the standard sizes available in market and company specification of the same.



Model making Facilities:

Models are key tool for visualizing ideas and to translate them. Esac has always been educating students’ community through physical models. All basic stationeries and model making materials are available at the institute. We provide students with materials for hands-on training in making models.
